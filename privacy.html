How we handle your data.

Fluent ("we", "us") cares about how your data is handled. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what you can do about it. Written in plain English.

Who we are

Fluent is operated by [YOUR COMPANY NAME LTD], registered in the United Kingdom (company number [NUMBER]), trading from [ADDRESS]. Data controller for purposes of UK GDPR.

What we collect

When you buy a Fluent skill or join our list:

  • Your email address (always)
  • Your name (if you provide one at checkout)
  • Payment information — handled directly by Stripe; we don't store card details
  • Your country / billing region (for VAT and analytics)
  • Basic analytics about how you arrived and what you looked at (page views, referrer)

What we use it for

  • Delivering your purchase. Your email is how we send you the files.
  • Customer support. Responding to your messages, fixing problems, processing refunds.
  • Sending you updates (only if you've opted in to the list). New skills, product notes, the occasional honest update on what we're building. Unsubscribe in any email.
  • Improving the product. Aggregated analytics about which skills are popular, which pages convert, which questions come up most.
  • Tax and legal compliance. Keeping order records for HMRC.

Who we share data with

Only the service providers we need to run the business:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Netlify — website hosting
  • Kit — email delivery
  • Google Workspace — for our own email

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with anyone for marketing purposes. We don't pass it to third parties beyond the providers above.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can:

  • Ask to see what data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct anything that's wrong
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal record-keeping requirements for tax)
  • Withdraw consent to marketing emails at any time (one-click unsubscribe)
  • Complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk if you think we've handled your data badly

Email hello@fluent.md for any of the above. We respond within 30 days as required by UK GDPR, usually faster.

How long we keep things

  • Order records — 6 years (HMRC requirement)
  • Marketing email list — until you unsubscribe
  • Customer support emails — 2 years
  • Analytics — aggregated and anonymised after 14 months

Cookies

We use the minimum necessary cookies to make the site work and the checkout reliable. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking beyond essential service providers (Stripe for payment, our email tool for confirmations). No banner because there's nothing to consent to that's not strictly necessary.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle your data we'll update this page and email subscribers. The "last updated" date at the top of the page always reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: hello@fluent.md.
